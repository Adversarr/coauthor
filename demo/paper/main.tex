\begin{abstract}
This paper explores the integration of large language models into collaborative academic writing workflows. We present CoAuthor, a system designed to assist researchers in drafting, refining, and iterating on scholarly documents. Our approach combines event-sourced architecture with intelligent agent workflows to enable transparent and reproducible collaborative writing.
\end{abstract}
